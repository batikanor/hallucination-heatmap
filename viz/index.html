About Hallucination Heatmap

This tool visualizes the "Trustworthiness Boundary" of Large Language Models.

By querying an LLM for statistical data (e.g., "What is the GDP of X?") and comparing it to ground truth, we generate a

  • Green: High Accuracy (< 5% Error)
  • Yellow: Moderate Drift (5-20% Error)
  • Red: Significant Hallucination (> 20% Error)
